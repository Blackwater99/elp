New Book Coming Soon

Set against the quiet backdrop of rural Nebraska, this upcoming novel explores the profound, realistic experiences of children navigating life with narcissistic parents. It is a story of resilience, reality, and finding one's own voice.

"Idy was forced to learn early how to read a room, how to soothe, how to shrink, how to disappear when needed. Some of those skills made her an excellent therapist, while others, like shrinking and disappearing, she was only able to leave in Bedford Springs as long as she lived in a different country, and all of those skills had made her a complicated daughter."
